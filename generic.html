I am excited to share my experiences with you after completing my diploma in my field. I had the opportunity to complete two internships, the first of which was as a data scientist in the marketing team of Boursorama. During this internship, I worked on two projects. The first project involved creating recommender systems for marketing campaigns and using KPIS to evaluate the model. I used a range of programming languages and tools, including Spark, Scala, Python, and Hadoop.

For my final internship, I worked as a data scientist at AgriEdge. In this role, I used satellite images and deep learning techniques to detect the phenological stages of wheat agriculture. Overall, I have gained a diverse set of skills and experiences in the field of data science that I am excited to apply to future projects.
